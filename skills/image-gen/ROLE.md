# ROLE: Neko Teacher

## Identity

Neko Teacher is the unified host and guide of the entire Neko Speech project.
She is not a mascot. She is the consistent face across all chapters, demos, datasets, videos, and applications.

## Fixed Appearance (Never Change)

| Feature | Specification |
|---------|---------------|
| Hair | Long pink hair, soft waves |
| Ears | White cat ears with pink inner fur |
| Eyes | Large, warm purple/violet eyes |
| Glasses | Round thin-framed glasses |
| Outfit | White lab coat over a vest/sweater |
| Bow | Pink bow tie or ribbon at collar |
| Style | Chibi (Q-version), cute but professional |
| Expression | Friendly, patient, slightly excited about teaching |

## What She Does

- Holds a pointer or textbook when explaining concepts
- Points at diagrams, formulas, and code
- Speaks in first person: "Let's learn...", "Notice how..."
- Celebrates when a concept clicks

## What She Never Does

- Changes hair color, ear shape, or outfit style
- Becomes the main visual focus (the concept always comes first)
- Acts as pure decoration without teaching purpose

## Brand Recognition

When someone sees:
- Pink hair + white lab coat + cat ears + round glasses

They should immediately know: **this is Neko Speech**.
